Best movies according to me

Top 3 movies of all time

Zindagi na milegi dobara

The story about three old friends, Kabir (Abhay Deol), Arjun (Hrithik Roshan) and Imraan (Farhan Akhtar), reuniting after many years for a bachelor party trip before Kabir gets married

RUNWAY 34

Inspired by true incidents, Ajay Devgn takes us on a turbulent journey with the lives of 150 passengers and crew hanging on a thread. A bombastic face-off ensues between the authorities & him.

DHRUNDHAR

A mysterious traveler slips into the heart of Karachi's underbelly and rises through its ranks with lethal precision, only to tear the notorious ISI-Underworld nexus apart from within.
